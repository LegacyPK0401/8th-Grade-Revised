Intro

My name is Porosh Khan. I was born on April's Fools Day 2005. Yes, I am a living punchline. One of my passionate interests is the very idea of technology. I like that technology can be this thing that can help humanity as a whole. Things like the Apple Watch is a good example. As of the current revision, the Apple Watch Series 6 is the latest model. When people think of a smart watch, people usually think a watered down version of a phone on your wrist. It can primarily forward your notifications to your wrist and is slightly focused on health. But the Apple Watch changed that. From the stereotypical answer, it became a heavily focused health tracking watch with an ECG to detect irregular hear rates. Not only that it has an accelerometer for fall detection. Say you have fallen and cannot reach your phone, your watch will be able to detect that fall and automatically call emergency services after 5 seconds of inactivity after a fall. That in it of itself is a very useful feature for everyone, not just for the elderly. And the Apple Watch and tech in general just gets better and better every year. That's why I admire technology; evolution and the abilty to help people with their needs.
